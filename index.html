Leverage big-data genomic studies through absorbing Markov chains to simultaneously group compounds de novo and learn novel drug-gene targets reinforced by drug-drug community structure. Utilizing these learned targets and previous annotations to identify potentially synthetic lethal genes which are frequently visited on random walks between synergistic drug combinations.
